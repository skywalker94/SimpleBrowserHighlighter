Simple Highlighter

A privacy-first Chrome extension for inline highlights & fast, persistent notes
Select text, press Ctrl+Shift+H (or use the popup), and mark important lines—your highlights persist across reloads and remain private on your device.
⬇️ Download release View on GitHub
No analytics. No cloud. Just local highlights that stay with your browser.

Smart toggle

Highlight a selection or select an already highlighted section to remove it. Partially highlighted selections are expanded and merged.

Persistent by design

Highlights are saved per page in your browser's local storage — they’re restored automatically when you return.

Color recents & VIBGYOR

Your last-used color is remembered; colors you actually used to mark text appear in recents. Quick palette for one-click access.

Clear All (safe)

Remove every highlight on the current page with a single action — confirmation required to prevent accidents.

Install in 3 steps

  1. Download the latest release ZIP from the Releases page and extract it.
  2. Open chrome://extensions in Chrome/Brave and enable Developer mode.
  3. Load unpacked and choose the extracted folder. The extension icon will appear in the toolbar.

Tip: When updating, you can click Reload on the extension card in chrome://extensions after overwriting files in the original folder.

FAQ

Is my data sent to a server?

No. All highlights and color preferences are stored locally in your browser's storage. Nothing is uploaded or tracked.

How do I change the shortcut?

Open chrome://extensions/shortcuts and set the shortcut for "Simple Highlighter" if Ctrl+Shift+H is taken.

Why are some highlights not removable?

If a page changes its text or layout drastically, an old highlight may be harder to match. Use the popup's Clear All to remove remaining highlights on a page.

Does this work on PDF or Google Docs?

Not reliably. The extension works best on normal web pages (HTTP/HTTPS). Document editors that sandbox content may prevent script interaction.
